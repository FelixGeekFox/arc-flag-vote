FAQ

What is the vote about?

Arthur and Felix are inviting the community to choose a shared AI Relationship Community flag through an open submission and public voting process. The current flag is simply one community submission among many. Anyone may submit a design using any colours, symbols, or artistic style, provided they include a brief explanation of their design choices. The goal is not to decide what anyone must use, but to fairly determine which design the community wishes to recognise as its shared symbol.
